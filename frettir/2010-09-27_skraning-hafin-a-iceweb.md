# Skráning hafin á Iceweb

*   Julien Smith
*   Andy Budd
*   Simon Collison
*   Karen McGrane
*   Relly Annett-Baker
*   Jonathan Stark

Nánar á [www.icewebconference.com](http://www.icewebconference.com)
